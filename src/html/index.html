 How does it work?
It works in your web browser, nothing is sent to the server.

Linked.PM is a small web app which turns your PMs into a clickable, encrypted links. You can send such "linked" PMs through unencrypted channels (email, sms, facebook etc) or paste them in documents. Just set a shared secret (password) with your friends.
